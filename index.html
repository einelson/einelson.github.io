Hi there, I am Ethan Nelson and I am currently a student at Brigham Young University-Idaho studying computer science.

    Recently I had the opportunity to do data manipulation with a file that was formatted terribly. I made a plan then faced the challenge head on. I was able to complete the challenge because I planned and kept my head on.

    I did research on the effectiveness of paired programming. We found that 2 people working in a team had more than 2x the output than if both people were working separately. This was because the team members were able to catch mistakes, combine knowledge and encourage each other.
